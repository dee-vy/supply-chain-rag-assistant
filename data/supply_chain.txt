Apple EMEIA Supply Chain Overview

Key logistics hubs for EMEIA operations include Dublin, Amsterdam, and Dubai.
Dublin serves as the primary distribution center for Western Europe.

Common delay risks include port congestion at Rotterdam, customs clearance
delays in Germany, and seasonal demand spikes in Q4.

Inventory management follows a Just-In-Time model to minimise warehousing costs.
Safety stock thresholds are reviewed quarterly based on demand forecasting models.

Supplier lead times vary between 2-8 weeks depending on component type.
Critical components such as display panels and chips have longer lead times of 6-8 weeks.

EMEIA logistics partners include DHL, Kuehne+Nagel, and DB Schenker.
